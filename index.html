About Us

Lincoln M. Blackwood, CHB was established in June 1999.

We provide customs brokerage, warehousing and other logistics services.

Licensed to clear cargo through U.S. Customs at all ports of entry in the USA. LMB handles a wide range of merchandise including perishables and wearing apparel and maintains a robust and healthy business relationship with numerous small, medium and large importers.
